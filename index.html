The Origin

The Story of Asteroid

Liv Perrotto was 15 years old with a dream that reached the stars. A passionate fan of Elon Musk and SpaceX, she designed a character that would become something far bigger than she ever imagined — Asteroid, the Shiba Inu astronaut.

Asteroid wasn't just a cute design. Liv's creation was selected as the official zero-gravity indicator aboard the SpaceX Polaris Dawn mission, floating weightlessly in the Dragon capsule as humanity pushed further into the cosmos.

Tragically, Liv passed away from cancer. Just days before, she had a chance to speak with Elon Musk — but she was too tired and asked him to call later. Her questions still sit on her nightstand, unanswered.

Glenn Beck shared Liv's story and her unanswered questions with Elon Musk, including her final wish: @GlennBeck to @ElonMusk — April 2026

  2. 2 Are you expanding the Tesla Diner to new areas?
